## 📖 **Description**

**EchoTrace** is an intelligent video retrieval system that uses AI to search through video footage using natural language queries. Instead of manually reviewing hours of surveillance or recorded videos, users can simply enter queries like *"Find a person wearing a red shirt"* or *"Show me the white car entering the parking lot."*

The system understands user queries using advanced **Natural Language Processing (NLP)** and **Vision-Language AI models**, analyzes recorded footage, and retrieves the most relevant video clips along with their exact timestamps.

---

## ✨ **Features**

- 🔍 **Natural Language Search** - Search videos using plain English queries
- 📤 **Video Upload** - Drag & drop or click to upload videos (MP4, AVI, MOV, MKV)
- 🎥 **Video Player** - Click results to play videos with timestamp navigation
- 📜 **Search History** - Track and revisit previous searches
- 🎯 **Smart Filtering** - Filter results by relevance, timestamp, or upload date
- 📊 **Confidence Scoring** - Each result shows a confidence percentage
- 🖤 **Cyberpunk/Black Theme** - Modern, dark UI with neon accents
- 📱 **Responsive Design** - Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile
